Uses for Hemp Oil for Dogs: 5 Things You Didn’t Know

  • admin
  • July 27, 2019
  • CBD For Pets
  • 0 Comments

Hemp oil for dogs is becoming increasingly popular among holistic veterinarians and pet owners seeking alternative, all-natural solutions for common pet health concerns. Though cannabidiol (CBD) has been gaining acceptance as a legitimate medical treatment for humans for the past decade, medical researchers are now discovering numerous health-enhancing benefits for pets, too. Here are five things about hemp oil for dogs that you might not have known.

1. Hemp and marijuana aren’t the same thing.

While the terms marijuana and hemp are often used interchangeably,
they are actually two varieties of cannabis sativa. Hemp doesn’t contain
significant amounts of the compound tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), which
is the element that delivers the intoxicating effect commonly associated
with marijuana consumption. What hemp does contain, however, is high
amounts of CBD, which is a compound used for its medical properties.

Hemp and marijuana plants are cultivated differently. Because hemp is used for a wide variety of industrial applications (clothing fibers, construction materials, paper), it is bred for sturdiness and can be grown easily in different climates. Marijuana is difficult to cultivate in certain climates, and has been bred to maximize production of trichomes that contain THC, which are negligible in hemp stalks.

2. Hemp oil CBD for dogs is not psychoactive.

CBD oil for dogs and humans does not contain the mind-altering
compound THC that is found in marijuana; neither you nor your pet will
become intoxicated from use of hemp oil for dogs (or people).

DO NOT give dogs any product containing THC – it is toxic to dogs.

3. Hemp oil for dogs may increase heart health.

Hemp oil for pets has been shown to help promote cardiovascular health by helping to slow damage to blood vessels, lower blood pressure, and even to regulate heart rhythm.

4. Hemp oil for dogs can help with allergy symptoms.

CBD has been found to be an anti-inflammatory agent
that can modify allergic responses at a cellular level. For pets, hemp
oil for allergies has been found to be an effective symptom reducer if
your pet is highly sensitive to certain man-made and/or environmental
elements.

5. Hemp oil for dogs helps alleviate dogs’ anxiety
